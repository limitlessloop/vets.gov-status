Performance by service

How is this measured?

Total Users: The count of total users to the various service lines such as Health Care, Education, Pension, etc. on VA.gov for the last 30 days.

User Satisfaction: An average user satisfaction score based on the feedback responses from users within the various service lines on VA.gov for the last 30 days.

Transactions: The total count of user actions such as submitting an application, downloading a form, scheduling an appointment as indicated by the specific tool, for the last 30 days.
